Frequently asked questions

Why won't my phone charge properly?

Usually it's a worn or dirty charging port rather than the battery. Ports loosen with years of plugging and unplugging. We'll diagnose it free and tell you whether it needs a clean or a new port.

How long does a charging port repair take?

Many charging port repairs are same-day. Some phones need the port soldered to the board, which takes longer — we'll confirm when we diagnose it.

Could it be the battery instead of the port?

Sometimes. That's why we run a free diagnosis first — so you only pay for the repair you actually need.
